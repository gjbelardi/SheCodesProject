After a few years of gradually recording in a friendsâ€™ basement in Queens, Gabriela Belardi (also known as Sullenflower) has finally made her artist debut with â€œShort Hair." Born and raised in South Salem, NY, Gabriela grew up running around the woods, swimming in lakes, and exhausting her energy through all things creative. Her father always playing guitar in his free time, and her mother an avid writer, she slowly came to mold both crafts together, and by the age of ten began writing lyrics to melodies in her head. She kept this hobby to herself for most of her childhood never considering it very serious, until taking a summer songwriting workshop at a local college, SUNY Purchase. Here she learned that not only was she very intrigued by songwriting, but she wanted to do it for the rest of her life. Gabriela then went on to study music at the City College of New York where she received a Bachelor of the Arts in Music. While in college she began performing at local venues and with friends in Brooklyn and Manhattan. After reconnecting with a high school friend, she began to record and form a band. Now, with the help of Ian McNally as engineer, her musical writings have reached the internet.
